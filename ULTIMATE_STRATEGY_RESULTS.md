**Why we didn't find it earlier:**
- Requires understanding of crash protection
- Counter-intuitive (allocate to "expensive" hedges)
- Universa-style approach (not common)

**Why it works:**
- 90% equity captures most upside
- 10% hedges protect downside
- Dynamic allocation (more hedge near highs)
- Result: SPY-like returns with HALF the drawdowns!

**Real-world track record:**
- Universa (hedge fund): Protected clients in 2020 crash
- 40/60 portfolio with tail hedge: Outperforms over full cycles

### **2. Portable Alpha is Underrated**

**What it does:**
- Gets SPY's beta (market exposure)
- Adds uncorrelated alpha (bonds, commodities)
- Result: Market returns + alpha

- Diversification across asset classes
- Momentum works in bonds/commodities too
- Low correlation = smoother returns
